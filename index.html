General Questions

What if I go over my limit of calls?
Answer:

We're pretty relaxed about this. We don't charge you any fee. If you go over by only a few calls then we won't mind. If every month you are constantly over your limit then we'll try to work out an agreement somewhere in the middle with you.

I don't know which plan to choose!
Answer:

During your first week of service we can calculate how many calls you are estimated to get. You are welcome to estimate low and we'll let you know if you need to bump up to the next bigger option at the end of the month.
